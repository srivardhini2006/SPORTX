# Stakeholder Analysis

## Primary Stakeholders

### Customers

Customers are the primary users of SPORTX. They use the platform to browse products, search for sports equipment, manage shopping carts, place orders, and track their purchases.

### Administrators

Administrators manage the overall platform by maintaining products, categories, inventory, and customer orders. They ensure smooth operation of the application.

---

## Secondary Stakeholders

### Developers

Developers design, implement, test, and maintain the SPORTX platform while ensuring scalability, security, and maintainability.

### Future Business Owners

As SPORTX evolves, business owners may use analytics and reports to make strategic decisions regarding products, customers, and business growth.

---

- Delivery Partners (Future)
